Your region is determined by your company headquarters. If your company's headquarters address is not in the region reflected here, you must change it by following the steps below.

Change region:
1. Navigate to the "Settings" app on your iPad.
2. Scroll down the Settings list on the left and tap on BoardWorks
3. Tap on "Region" which can be found in the "Configuration" section
4. You can now change the region to one of the four options:
    Australia (Asia-Pac)
    Canada
    United Kingdom (Africa, Europe, Middle East)
    United States

If unsure of your proper setting, please verify with your BoardWorks Administrator or cgsglobalsupport@computershare.com before modifying your region setting.
